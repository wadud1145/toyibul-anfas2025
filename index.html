OUR VISION AND MISSION.

INTRODUCTION

Toyibul Anfas Foundation is an NGO incoporated under the company code 1963, Act 179 to provide educational and other relevant aid to its host community.

The NGO before its registration on 8th June 2018 has been in existence for 5 years providing Islamic (Arabic) and circular tuition in habitants of Kasoa Cp. Students population has been increased overtime coupled with the need to provide quality and affordable to the poor and needy in the society.

To accomplish the above, Toyibul Anfas is soliciting for aid to make education accessible in the area.

MISSION

To provide quality accessible and affordable English and Arabic education for the less privileged children of Kasoa Cp and its environs.

VISION

To provide quality and affordable basic education for the inhabitants of Kasoa and Central Region at large in future.

OBJECTIVES

To achieve the above mission and vission, Toyibul Anfas wish to solicit support from benevolent organisation for the following

- Expansion of the school infrastructure (building and furniture) to be able to support the increasing student.

-Educational materials to support teaching and learning

- Feeding grant to enhance student's well-being and facilitate learning.

MANAGEMENT

Toyibul Anfas has committed and friendly administrative staff. The institution also employs qualified teachers from Ghana Education Service (GES) to ensure quality tuition.

OUR CUSTOMERS

Students and less privillege living around Kasoa.

COMPETETIVE ADVANTAGE

The community is inundated with institutions offering the English circular giving the school an advantage over other institutions and further advancing Islamic knowledgeable and eiquette to both Muslims and Non-Muslims.
